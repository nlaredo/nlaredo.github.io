How to Play

Based on WORDLE by Josh Wardle.

You have six chances to guess the WORD.

Your guess can be any valid five letter word.

Example

W
O
R
L
D

In this example, the letters "W" and "O" and "R" are all in the word and in their correct positions.

The letter "D" is in the word, but not in the correct position.

The letter "L" is not present at any position in the word.

Winning

You have won when all the tiles turn green after a guess:

W
O
R
D
S
You can wait for a new puzzle each day or just use the left and right arrows above the puzzle board to switch to a different day's puzzle.

Keyboard Statistics

S
12%

The percentages shown under each letter on the on screen keyboard represent the percentage of words in the dictionary that have that letter in that position in the word.

Once you enter a guess, the pool of words considered will shrink according to which guesses are marked correct or incorrect or in the wrong spot.

The colors of the keys will change to match the status of each letter as discovered in past guesses for the puzzle.
